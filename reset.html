RESET 2018 & 2019

A one-day cyber security conference with an all female speaker line-up. Over 175 attendees, gender-balanced from both junior and senior levels of industry. Expert speakers who provided in-depth knowledge of destructive cyberattacks and criminal operations, threat hunting and strategy, and human centric security. An event to disrupt the industry standard and highlight the contributions and presence of women in cybersecurity - and reset the balance.
